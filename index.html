About Me

A bit more about me...

Based in Malaga, with a Hospitality background and a degree in Tourism, my curiosity about coding started last year when I was an SDR for a company developing websites for Hotels called Paraty Tech. During my campaigns getting leads from Spain, Colombia, Costa Rica, Panamá and EE.UU; I started getting involved in the needs of our clients providing them with information & an excellent customer service.

Passionate about coding...

I graduated in a Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp at Adalab school, where I built real-world projects using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js. Throughout the program, I strengthened my problem-solving skills, learned to work with APIs and databases, and developed a strong understanding of both frontend and backend architecture. I am particularly passionate about frontend development, creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces. I am continuously learning and improving my skills, currently expanding my knowledge in TypeScript and modern development practices.
